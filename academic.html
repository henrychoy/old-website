Academic Background

Graduate Program - Boston University

Masters of Science in Software Development

I am currently in the Masters of Science in Software Development program at BU Metropolitan College. I've always had an interest in technology and programming ever since I took an intro to java class in undergrad, and with this degree I'm hoping to establish a foundation of knowledge that will enable me to create applications that can make a difference in people's lives. Below are the classes I've taken so far.

  • CS300 - Introduction to Software Development
  • CS521 - Information Structures with Python
  • CS682 - Information Systems Analysis and Design
  • CS526 - Data Structures and Algorithms
  • CS622 - Advanced Programming Techniques
  • CS665 - Software Design and Patterns
  • CS669 - Database Design and Implementation for Business
  • CS601 - Web Application Development
  • CS602 - Server-Side Web Development

Undergraduate Program - Boston University

Questrom School of Business - Bachelors of Science in Business Administration

I completed my undergrad at BU Questrom School of Business with a concentration in finance and management information systems. The business program provided a broad overview of many business disciplines and culminated in a semester of integrated "CORE" classes in junior year of finance, operations management, marketing, and information systems. I also took many electives from all types of subjects such as creative writing, psychology, ethics, and music theory.
