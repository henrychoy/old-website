Emergency Medical Technician

I worked in a variety of side-jobs throughout the years, but the most interesting one is definitely working as an EMT at Fallon Ambulance Service. Fallon provides emergency services and transportation to over 150,000 patients annually in Brookline, Dedham, Milton, Weymouth, and the Boston-metro area. I worked the overnight 11pm - 7am shift on Fridays, on top of my normal full-time job. Although the job was stressful, I learned alot and have the upmost respect to all first-responders and emergency personnel.
